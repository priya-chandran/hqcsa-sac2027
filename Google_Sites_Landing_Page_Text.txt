Page title:
Hybrid Quantum-Classical Systems and Architectures (HQCSA)

Subtitle:
ACM SAC 2027 Track

Main text:
Welcome to the inaugural HQCSA track at ACM SAC 2027. HQCSA provides a forum for research on hybrid quantum-classical systems, architectures, infrastructures, fault-tolerant computing, security, AI-assisted quantum systems, and real-world applications.

Practical quantum computing will be hybrid. HQCSA focuses on the systems and architectures layer that will make quantum computing scalable, reliable, secure, and deployable.
